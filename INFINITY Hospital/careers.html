Senior NICU Staff Nurse

Department: NICU & Neonatal Care • Experience: 3+ Years • Type: Full-Time
+
Job Overview

We are seeking compassionate, detail-oriented Staff Nurses to handle high-acuity newborn monitoring in our Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU).

Responsibilities
  • Provide continuous monitoring of newborn vitals and neonatal incubators.
  • Assist pediatric neonatologists in emergency stabilization, ventilator settings, and NICU care.
  • Maintain sterile standards across specialized drug administrations, feeding, and incubator management.
Requirements
  • B.Sc Nursing or GNM certification from an accredited nursing council.
  • Active nursing registration license.
  • Sound clinical knowledge of neonatal and NICU care protocols.

Resident Obstetrician / Gynecologist

Department: Pregnancy & Maternity Care • Experience: 1+ Year • Type: Shift Rotations
+
Job Overview

We are looking for Resident Obstetricians / Gynecologists to manage active maternity admissions, support comfort-focused deliveries, and assist the senior obstetrician during C-sections.

Responsibilities
  • Conduct initial evaluations in our comfortable birthing suites and monitor fetal heart rates.
  • Assist senior consultants during deliveries, maternity care, and fertility procedures.
  • Coordinate postnatal recovery checkups and care plans for families.
Requirements
  • MS (OBG) or DGO Degree from a recognized medical institution.
  • Active state medical registration.
  • Strong clinical communication and labor monitoring skills.

Embryology Lab Coordinator

Department: IVF & Fertility Solutions • Experience: 2+ Years • Type: Full-Time
+
Job Overview

We are hiring an Embryology Lab Coordinator to manage quality controls, equipment calibration, and inventory within our advanced embryology laboratory.

Responsibilities
  • Coordinate workflow schedules, verify sterile conditions, and maintain logs in our embryology laboratory.
  • Monitor gas levels, incubator temperatures, and performance metrics of advanced laboratory systems.
  • Work alongside chief embryologists to ensure treatment data accuracy, sample security, and compliance standards.
Requirements
  • B.Sc or M.Sc in Life Sciences, Biotechnology, or equivalent clinical laboratory experience.
  • Prior experience in a clinical IVF or embryology laboratory environment.
  • Base computer operating literacy for equipment logs and inventories.
